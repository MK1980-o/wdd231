Harare Attractions

Harare Gardens

The city's largest public park located in the central business district. Features beautiful gardens, walking paths, and recreational facilities.

Park & Recreation City Center

National Gallery of Zimbabwe

Premier art gallery showcasing Zimbabwean and international contemporary art. Home to the famous Stone Sculpture collection.

Cultural City Center

Epworth Balancing Rocks

Unique geological formations that have become a symbol of Zimbabwe. Featured on the country's currency notes.

Natural Wonder 15km SE of Harare

Mbare Musika Market

Harare's largest and most vibrant market. Experience local culture, fresh produce, crafts, and traditional medicines.

Market & Shopping Mbare Township

Chapungu Sculpture Park

Open-air gallery showcasing magnificent stone sculptures by Zimbabwean artists in a beautiful natural setting.

Art & Culture Msasa

Harare Botanical Gardens

68 hectares of themed gardens featuring plants from around the world, with a focus on Zimbabwean flora.

Nature & Gardens Alexandra Park

About Harare

Climate

Harare has a subtropical highland climate with warm summers and mild, sunny winters. The city enjoys pleasant weather year-round.

Economy

Harare is Zimbabwe's financial and commercial hub, hosting the Zimbabwe Stock Exchange and major banking institutions.

Culture

The city blends modern urban life with rich Zimbabwean traditions, offering diverse culinary, musical, and artistic experiences.
